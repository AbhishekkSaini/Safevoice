Real incidents shared by survivors

P

Protected Identity

Delhi, 2023

I was returning from my evening tuition classes when it happened. Thanks to a nearby PCR van and alert citizens, the perpetrators were caught. The support from my family and NGO counselors helped me fight the legal battle. Today, I advocate for better street lighting and security in our area.

#Justice
S

Survivor

Mumbai, 2022

It was my workplace, where I should have felt safe. After months of harassment, I finally gathered courage to file a complaint. The internal committee took strict action, and now our organization has stronger policies. Remember, silence only empowers the perpetrators.

#Workplace
R

Rising Voice

Bangalore, 2023

I was drugged at a party by someone I trusted. The trauma left me broken, but with therapy and support groups, I'm rebuilding my life. Now I conduct awareness sessions about consent and safety in colleges. Your voice matters, speak up.

#Awareness
